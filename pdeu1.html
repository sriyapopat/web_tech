About Us

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) is an autonomous institution, located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. It was established with the vision to impart quality education and research in the field of energy and related disciplines.

Vision: To become a globally recognized center of excellence in energy and technology.

Mission: To foster innovative research, education, and solutions for energy sustainability.
