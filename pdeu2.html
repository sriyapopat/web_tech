About Us

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) is located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. It aims to provide quality education and research in energy and related fields.

Vision: To become a globally recognized center of excellence in energy and technology.

Mission: To foster innovative research and solutions for energy sustainability.

Departments

  • Department of Electrical Engineering
  • Department of Mechanical Engineering
  • Department of Computer Science and Engineering
  • Department of Civil Engineering
  • Department of Renewable Energy
